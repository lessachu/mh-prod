For the second extraction, note that Willy jumps over at least one sea wall in each grid and that each sea wall has a child standing on it. Take the letter that corresponds to where the child is standing on the sea wall, from Willy's point of view. (E.g., in the second grid, Jonah stands at the east edge of his wall as Willy jumps from the South. Jonah's as far as he can be to Willy's right, so we extract H. Heath stands at the middle of his wall, so we extract A.) Reading in jump-order gets the final word: SHACKLE
