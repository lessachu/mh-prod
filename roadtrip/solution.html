How to Solve

The puzzle is easiest if you notice the first letters of the authors at bottom spell out a long message: “The sixteen authors’ books each clue an emoji pair. Go very close to and use zappar app at each location plaque. Collect pixel figures from the newly clued authors.“

You'll then need to download the Zappar AR application and scan the Zappar app icon for this puzzle:

At each of the locations, search for each author on loan at the specified location by zooming in on the room number plaque via the Zappar app. Looking around in Zappar, you'll find the following emoji which clue a book by another author. Selecting that author in the puzzle page produces a pixel map. Collating the pixel maps according to the virtual location of the emoji sequences in Zappar gives you the grid location for the corresponding pxel map (these are indicated in parentheses in the table below).
