For this event, teams received a call on Friday afternoon instructing them to send one team member to the Robot parade dressed as one of the following robots, all from Futurama: Bender, Crushinator, Malfunctioning Eddie, Angelyne or Calculon. We did not systematically bucket teams and ended up with an excess of Benders. Overall, Left Out was extremely impressed at the caliber of the costumes on display during the event.
